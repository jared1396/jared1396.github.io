Smiley Jose

You bumble, you stumble, and a lot of times crumble but you always do it with a smile on your face and unending energy. Here’s the thing - people like you a lot. And you’ve been pretty good for your life, no murders on your hands.

But you do dabble in the illegal - tax evasion specifically. You also know a lot of hackers, having been part of an elite hacker group in college. Your Secret: There’s a rumor that you hired the hacker to release President Grump’s peen. It's true.
